Biochemistry Metabolic Network

Exploring complex metabolic pathways and molecular interactions within cells

Network Overview

The biochemistry metabolic network is a collection of all metabolic reactions within cells, involving thousands of enzymes, substrates, and products in complex interactions. This network visualization shows the dynamic relationships between these molecules.

Carbohydrate Metabolism

Amino acid metabolism, urea cycle, nucleotide biosynthesis, purine and pyrimidine metabolism, etc.
